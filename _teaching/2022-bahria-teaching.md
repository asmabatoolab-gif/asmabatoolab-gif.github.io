Served as Visiting Lecturer in the Department of International Relations at Bahria University, Islamabad from March to September 2022.

## Responsibilities

- Delivered undergraduate lectures in International Relations theory and practice
- Designed course materials, syllabi, and assessment criteria from scratch
- Conducted student evaluations and provided academic mentorship
- Covered topics including South Asian geopolitics, foreign policy analysis, and diplomatic studies

## Topics Covered

- Theories of International Relations (Realism, Liberalism, Constructivism)
- South Asian regional politics and security
- Pakistan's foreign policy
- Diplomacy and international negotiations
- Soft power and public diplomacy
